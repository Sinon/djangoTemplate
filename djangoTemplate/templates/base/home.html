What is [tosp] & [the tospians]

A geek band for the universe

"[tosp] & [the tospians]" is a band of creators. We focus on creating projects, that are fun, exciting, and that challenge us intelectually, but above everything else we ask one question before embarking on a project:

Is this something really cool that should exist?

The way we work closely resembles a jazz improv band. We set a list of projects that we want to create over the course of a year. And during that year we work on whatever we feel like doing, with the only caveat being that all projects should be finished before the end of the cycle.

This cycle's deadline is Oct. 1, 2017.

Stay tuned to learn more about our projects.
